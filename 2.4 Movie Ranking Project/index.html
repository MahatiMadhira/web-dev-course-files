The Best Movies according to Mahati

My top 3 movies of all time

Parasite

The symbolism throughout the movie to show the economic disparity betweeen the two families is excellent.

RRR

This is a South-Indian movie set during the British rule over the country. It follows two leads fighting for their own causes that seem different but are the same.

Shawshank Redemption

This movie is an absolute classic of great writing and amazing acting.
